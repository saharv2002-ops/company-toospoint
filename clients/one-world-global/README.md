# One World Global (OWG)

## Engagement (proposed)

- **Model:** Fractional consulting
- **Retainer:** $6,000 USD / month
- **Time commitment:** 25–30 hours / week
- **Proposed start:** June 1, 2026
- **Terms:** Net 15
- **Status:** Proposal delivered

## Scope of the proposed engagement

Four interlocking focus areas:

1. **Interpreter Recruitment** — pipeline strategy, sourcing, language-demand alignment
2. **Interpreter Engagement & Care** — retention, communication, onboarding experience
3. **Operational Support & Scaling** — workflows, coverage optimization, vendor structure
4. **Client Acquisition & Growth** — positioning, onboarding readiness, go-to-market capacity
