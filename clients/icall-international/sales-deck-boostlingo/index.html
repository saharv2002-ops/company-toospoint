Compliance you can audit, not just trust.

Four layers of independent and recurring verification on every interpreter on our bench — before they take a call and every month after.

01 · Third-Party Language Testing
Independent linguistic verification
Every interpreter is tested by external assessors — not graded internally — so language proficiency is documented by a neutral third party Boostlingo can verify.
Vendors · Alta · LanguageStat
02 · Monthly Sanctions Screening
Re-screened every month against SDN & OIG
All active interpreters are rerun monthly against the OFAC SDN list and the OIG List of Excluded Individuals/Entities (LEIE). Anyone who appears is removed from the active bench immediately.
Cadence · Monthly · 100% of active bench
03 · Background Checks
Standardized pre-engagement screening
Background checks are run through ESS for every interpreter prior to being routed live calls — applied consistently across the bench, with results retained on file.
Vendor · ESS
04 · Employer Reference Verification
HR-led correspondence with prior employers
Our HR team directly contacts previous employers and language-service providers to verify experience and gather first-hand performance feedback — not just resume review.

03 / 07

Quality assurance, before the first live call.

Every interpreter on our bench has cleared the same five-pillar onboarding gate before being routed a single call.

01
Linguistic Fluency Testing
Source- and target-language proficiency assessed against a documented rubric, with independent third-party scoring on top.
02
OPI & VRI Protocol Training
Mandatory training on remote interpretation protocols — turn-taking, message management, sight translation, VRI camera etiquette.
03
Cultural Competency Evaluation
Evaluated on cultural appropriateness, register, and the judgment calls that separate fluent speakers from working interpreters.
04
Certification Verification
Where certifications are claimed (e.g., CCHI, NBCMI, court-certified), we verify each one directly with the issuing body before activation.
05
Background Checks
Full ESS background screening completed and on file before an interpreter is routed any live call.
Onboarding Gate
An interpreter is only added to the active routing bench once all five pillars are documented and signed off — no provisional or partial activation.
