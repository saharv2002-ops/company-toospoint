%% ── Executive Summary ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Executive Summary}

I am pleased to submit this proposal to partner with \textbf{Language Group
Provider LLC} (``LGP'') on a fractional basis, focused on strengthening the
operational and compliance foundation required to transition LGP from a
subcontractor model into a credible direct vendor for U.S.\ healthcare and
government clients.

The next stage of LGP's growth is not gated by demand. With 300+ certified
interpreters across LATAM and the U.S.\ and active subcontracted volume
through Propio, OWGS, and Language Arts, the language coverage and delivery
muscle already exist. What is required to win directly with hospitals, public
agencies, and enterprise healthcare buyers is a tightened operational,
compliance, and quality posture --- the posture those buyers expect before
they ever issue a contract.

\begin{clausebox}
\sffamily\small
\textbf{The thesis:} LGP's transition from subcontractor to direct vendor is
gated less by client appetite and more by the operational, compliance, and
QA infrastructure expected of a direct healthcare and government partner.
This engagement is structured to build that infrastructure first, then
convert it into a direct-client growth engine.
\end{clausebox}

%% ── Engagement Overview ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Engagement Overview}

I will provide \textbf{fractional operational and growth support} for
Language Group Provider, anchored across four interlocking focus areas. The
areas are designed to run together, but the engagement is deliberately
sequenced --- foundation first, then scalable service model, then direct
client growth.

\vspace{4pt}
\pillar{1. Operational Foundation}{Workflows, vendor and interpreter structure, onboarding, and lifecycle management}
\pillar{2. Compliance \& QA Framework}{CMS, HIPAA, and FWA readiness; QA processes; audit-ready documentation}
\pillar{3. Scalable Service Model}{Interpreter training, readiness, retention, and quality consistency at scale}
\pillar{4. Direct Client Acquisition \& RFP Positioning}{Pipeline build, RFP readiness, positioning, and win-rate improvement in healthcare and government}
\vspace{4pt}

This engagement is designed to operate \textbf{inside} LGP's day-to-day
rather than alongside it --- producing tangible operational outputs, not just
recommendations.

%% ── Scope of Support ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Scope of Support}

\subsection{1. Operational Foundation}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=3pt, topsep=2pt]
  \item Review and tightening of operational workflows across the
        interpreter and request lifecycle
  \item Vendor and interpreter management structure recommendations
        (roles, accountability, escalation paths)
  \item Onboarding process improvement (time-to-first-call, completion
        rates, documentation hygiene)
  \item Queue, dispatch, and coverage process guidance aligned to LGP's
        current and target language mix
  \item Internal communication and escalation workflows that hold up under
        direct-client SLAs
\end{itemize}

\subsection{2. Compliance \& QA Framework}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=3pt, topsep=2pt]
  \item Compliance readiness review aligned to healthcare and government
        expectations (CMS, HIPAA, FWA)
  \item QA framework development --- call monitoring, scoring rubrics,
        corrective action loops, and interpreter feedback cycles
  \item Documentation and audit-readiness posture: policies, training
        records, vendor records, attestations
  \item Interpreter credentialing and re-credentialing workflow
        recommendations
  \item Risk identification and remediation roadmap, prioritized by what
        direct buyers actually evaluate
\end{itemize}

\subsection{3. Scalable Service Model}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=3pt, topsep=2pt]
  \item Interpreter training and readiness program structure ---
        healthcare and government specializations
  \item Quality consistency across languages and channels (OPI, VRI,
        on-site as applicable)
  \item Interpreter engagement, communication, and retention practices
        that hold quality steady as volume grows
  \item Performance monitoring and continuous-improvement loops
  \item Service-model design that supports consistent client experience
        once direct accounts come online
\end{itemize}

\subsection{4. Direct Client Acquisition \& RFP Positioning}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=3pt, topsep=2pt]
  \item Positioning and messaging tuned to healthcare and government
        buyers --- moving LGP's narrative from subcontractor to direct
        vendor
  \item RFP readiness package: capability statement, past performance
        framing, compliance attestations, and standardized response assets
  \item RFP response support --- structure, narrative, and review on
        priority opportunities
  \item Direct client pipeline strategy: target segmentation, outreach
        sequencing, and conversion path
  \item Onboarding readiness for new direct clients (capacity, language
        coverage, SLA posture, reporting)
\end{itemize}

%% ── Approach ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Approach \& Working Model}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=4pt, topsep=4pt]
  \item \textbf{Foundation first.} The engagement is sequenced so that
        operational, compliance, and QA gaps are closed before LGP scales
        direct-client outreach --- protecting both reputation and
        retention.
  \item \textbf{Embedded, not external.} I operate as a fractional
        extension of LGP's team --- participating in workflows, not
        observing them.
  \item \textbf{Outcome-led.} Each focus area is tracked against agreed
        operational signals (e.g., compliance readiness, QA scores,
        interpreter retention, RFP win rate, direct pipeline).
  \item \textbf{Cadence-driven.} Weekly working sessions, monthly
        priority reviews, and asynchronous execution between touchpoints.
  \item \textbf{Adaptive scope.} The engagement adjusts to operational
        priorities and live RFP opportunities as they evolve through the
        quarter.
\end{itemize}
