# Language Group Provider LLC (LGP)

## Engagement (proposed)

- **Model:** Fractional consulting — ongoing strategic partnership, reviewed quarterly
- **Retainer:** $5,000 USD / month
- **Time commitment:** 40 hours / month
- **Proposed start:** July 1, 2026
- **Terms:** Net 15
- **Status:** Proposal drafted 2026-06-08, not yet signed

## Primary contact

- **Norman** — owner / decision-maker

## What we know about LGP

- 300+ certified interpreters across **LATAM and the U.S.**
- Languages: Spanish, Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Ukrainian, Portuguese
- Currently subcontracting through **Propio, OWGS, Language Arts**
- Strategic intent: scale **beyond subcontracting** and become a direct vendor to U.S. hospitals, public agencies, and enterprise healthcare buyers
- Compliance focus areas they're building toward: **CMS, HIPAA, FWA**

## Scope of the proposed engagement

Four interlocking focus areas, sequenced foundation-first:

1. **Operational Foundation** — workflows, vendor/interpreter structure, onboarding
2. **Compliance & QA Framework** — CMS / HIPAA / FWA readiness, QA processes, audit-ready docs
3. **Scalable Service Model** — interpreter training, readiness, retention, quality consistency
4. **Direct Client Acquisition & RFP Positioning** — pipeline, RFP readiness, win rate in healthcare and government
